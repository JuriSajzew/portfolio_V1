About me

Hello, I am a frontend developer based in Stuttgart, Germany. In my previous job as a logistics manager, I often came into contact with programming. I have always been fascinated by the technology and logic behind a user interface. My goal is to develop innovative solutions that exceed the expectations of my customers and at the same time have a positive impact on the users.
During my training at Delevolper Academy, I have built several projects. This allowed me to build up my expertise in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Angular, and TypeScript. Some projects had to be done in a team, which was a lot of fun.
I like to explore design trends and experiment with new techniques in my free time. I also work with new technologies to continuously expand my knowledge.
